About Yoga
Yoga promotes physical and mental health. First of all, yoga must be practiced, focusing on the combination of mind and body, with the characteristics of mental focus, movement and static, and letting go; secondly, yoga is different from the physical activity of large muscle groups in the whole body, and has the characteristics of body openness; third, with others Exercise is different. For example, fitness exercise aims to improve the physical function of the body, while yoga focuses on the development of the spirit on the basis of improving physical function. These characteristics enable yoga to better meet people's needs for improving physical and mental health. Modern medicine proves that yoga can effectively regulate the human nervous system and endocrine system, thereby improving physical health and giving people great spiritual enjoyment. The good psychological effects brought by yoga will inevitably have a positive impact on people's subsequent psychological development. Practice has proved that long-term adherence to yoga has an obvious promotion effect on people's mental health, and can significantly improve bad emotions such as confusion, fatigue, anxiety, depression, and anger.
